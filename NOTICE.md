# Acceptable Use Notice

Autody is published for first-party creator analytics: analyzing Douyin videos, comments, transcripts, and creator-center metrics that the user owns or is authorized to manage.

Do not use Autody to access, analyze, scrape, or infer private or non-public information from accounts, videos, dashboards, or browser sessions that you do not own or have permission to operate. Any misuse, including analysis of other people's videos or accounts without authorization, is solely the responsibility of the user.

Users are responsible for complying with applicable law, platform terms, privacy obligations, and data retention rules.
